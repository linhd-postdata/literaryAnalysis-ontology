Introduction back to ToC

The ontology of the Postdata Elements of Literary Analysis covers aspects related with the literary analysis of a poetic work. These aspects refer to rhetorical analysis, literary resources and other literary devices such as intertextuality. Therefore, the following classes are defined in this ontology: Acrostic, Intertextuality and FigureOfSpeech classes. This last class is the basis of a hierarchy of classes formed by Scheme and Trope and represents the main categorization of Figure of Speech used in rhetoric. The data and objects properties are described in the following sections.
